# PRODUCT SPEC — Dekorfabrik Website Redesign

## Product vision
Transform Dekorfabrik from a simple one-page informational site into a modern, trustworthy, conversion-oriented website for DACH customers needing vectorization and embroidery digitization services.

## Target audience
- Small and medium businesses in Germany, Austria, Switzerland.
- Design studios and print/embroidery partners.
- Buyers seeking reliable turnaround, transparent process, and high-quality output.

## Primary goals
1. Increase qualified inbound leads.
2. Improve trust through social proof, quality examples, and legal clarity.
3. Reduce friction in quote/upload workflow.
4. Build SEO/GEO-ready content foundation for long-term growth.

## MVP scope
- Home page with clear value proposition.
- Services overview page.
- FAQ page.
- Contact page with file upload capability.
- Legal pages: Impressum, Datenschutz, AGB placeholder.
- Basic SEO metadata and crawlability setup.

## Non-MVP (phased)
- Service-specific deep pages.
- Dynamic pricing calculators.
- Resource center/blog with structured taxonomy.
- Customer account portal.
- Advanced marketing automation and retargeting.

## Success metrics
- Upload form conversion rate.
- Qualified leads per month.
- CTR on primary CTAs.
- Organic traffic growth for service-intent pages.
- Improvement in Core Web Vitals and mobile usability.
